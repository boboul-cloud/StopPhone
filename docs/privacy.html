Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

🔒 No data collected

Short version: StopPhone does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything runs 100% on your device. No account, no analytics, no network requests.

1. Who we are

StopPhone is a free, open-source iOS application developed by boboul-cloud. The source code is publicly available at github.com/boboul-cloud/StopPhone.

2. Data we collect

We collect no data whatsoever.

Specifically, StopPhone does not:

  • Collect or store your GPS location or speed
  • Record which apps you use or block
  • Track your driving sessions or routes
  • Send any information to any external server
  • Use analytics or crash-reporting SDKs
  • Display advertising

3. Permissions used

StopPhone requests the following device permissions, used exclusively on-device:

  • Location (Always) — to measure your GPS speed in the background. Speed values are computed in real-time and never stored beyond the current app session.
  • Family Controls (Screen Time) — to block selected apps via Apple's managed settings API. No information about blocked apps is transmitted.
  • Notifications — to alert you when driving is detected. Notifications are local only (no push server involved).

4. Local storage (UserDefaults)

The app stores a small set of preferences locally on your device using Apple's UserDefaults (standard iOS mechanism):

  • Whether protection is enabled
  • Your chosen speed threshold
  • Your Bluetooth device preference
  • The selected apps/categories to block (stored as an encoded FamilyActivitySelection)

This data never leaves your device.

5. No third-party services

StopPhone contains no third-party SDKs, no advertising networks, no analytics libraries, and makes zero network requests. There is no backend server.

6. Children's privacy

StopPhone does not knowingly collect information from children. As no data is collected from anyone, this applies equally to all users regardless of age.

7. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated to reflect changes in the app's features. The "last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, open an issue on GitHub: github.com/boboul-cloud/StopPhone/issues
