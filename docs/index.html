Features

Everything you need to drive safely

StopPhone combines GPS speed, Bluetooth detection, and Screen Time to keep your eyes on the road.

🚗

GPS Speed Detection

Automatically activates above a configurable speed threshold (default 15 km/h). Anti-flutter hysteresis prevents false triggers.

🔵

Car Bluetooth Trigger

Starts blocking the moment your car's hands-free system connects. Supports any HFP / A2DP Bluetooth device or a specific pinned car.

📵

Screen Time Blocking

Uses Apple's Family Controls API to block apps and entire categories (Social, Games, Entertainment…). Or total block mode for zero distraction.

🔴

Full-screen Overlay

A hard-to-ignore red alert covers the screen while driving, with a confirmation dialog to dismiss it.

🔔

Local Notification

Sends a notification when driving is detected — with "Open StopPhone" / "Keep blocking" action buttons.

🔊

Voice Alert

Speaks an alert through your car speakers via Bluetooth audio, so you hear it even without looking at your phone.

⚡

Shortcuts Automation

URL scheme stopphone://activate lets you build a Siri Shortcut that launches StopPhone automatically when your car Bluetooth connects.

🌍

French & English

Fully localised in French and English. The app detects your device language automatically.

How it works

Simple. Automatic. Reliable.

No interaction needed while driving — StopPhone handles everything in the background.

1

Enable protection once

Flip the toggle on the dashboard. Grant location (Always) and Family Controls permissions. That's it.

2

Get in your car

As soon as your car Bluetooth connects or your GPS speed exceeds the threshold, StopPhone activates automatically — even if the app is closed.

3

Apps are blocked

Selected apps are shielded by Screen Time. A full-screen red overlay appears. Your car speakers announce the alert. A notification is sent.

4

Auto-disables when you stop

When your speed drops below the threshold (with a 5 km/h hysteresis buffer) or your car Bluetooth disconnects, blocking is removed automatically.

Shortcuts

Full automation with Siri Shortcuts

Use the URL scheme to trigger StopPhone from any automation, even when the app is closed.

stopphone://activate
stopphone://deactivate
1

Open the Shortcuts app

2

Automation → + → Personal Automation

3

Choose Bluetooth → your car → Connects

4

Add action "Open URLs" → stopphone://activate

Repeat with "Disconnects" → stopphone://deactivate for auto-disabling too.
